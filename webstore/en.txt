Turn every new tab into a calming nature escape with stunning bird wallpapers, soothing bird songs, and fascinating wildlife facts.

BirdTab replaces your Chrome new tab page with breathtaking bird photography from around the world. Every time you open a tab, you'll discover a new bird species - complete with authentic bird calls, nature sounds, and interesting facts. It's like having a window to the natural world right in your browser.

WHY USERS LOVE BIRDTAB

Unlike generic wallpaper extensions, BirdTab is a complete nature experience. Each new tab brings a moment of calm. A beautiful bird photograph, a relaxing bird call, and something new to learn. It's the perfect blend of mindfulness, education, and aesthetic beauty.

KEY FEATURES

- New bird wallpaper with every new tab - stunning high-resolution wildlife photography
- Authentic bird calls and nature sounds - with adjustable volume and quiet hours
- Fascinating bird facts - learn about behavior, habitat, and conservation status
- Global birding - explore birds from around the world, with more regions on the way
- Interactive bird identification quiz - test your knowledge and share results
- Viewing history - track every bird you've discovered
- Clock and countdown timer - stay focused while enjoying nature
- Productivity tools built in - search box, custom shortcuts, and more
- Works offline - enjoy your birds even without internet
- No ads, no sign-ups, no strings attached
- 100% privacy-focused - no personal data collected, no tracking

THOUSANDS OF SPECIES

Discover thousands of bird species from every continent - from tropical parrots and hummingbirds to majestic eagles and owls. Every photo is high-resolution and carefully curated to bring you the most stunning and diverse birding experience possible.
