Turn every new tab into a calming nature escape with stunning bird wallpapers, soothing bird songs, and fascinating wildlife facts.

BirdTab replaces your Chrome new tab page with breathtaking bird photography and videos from around the world. Every time you open a tab, you'll discover a new bird species - complete with authentic bird calls, nature sounds, and interesting facts. It's like having a window to the natural world right in your browser.

WHY USERS LOVE BIRDTAB

Unlike generic wallpaper extensions, BirdTab is a complete nature experience. Each new tab brings a moment of calm. A beautiful bird photograph, a relaxing bird call, and something new to learn. It's the perfect blend of mindfulness, education, and aesthetic beauty.

KEY FEATURES

- New bird wallpaper with every new tab - stunning wildlife photography in your browser
- HD bird videos - watch birds soar, sing, and forage in their natural habitats
- Authentic bird calls and nature sounds - with adjustable volume and quiet hours
- Fascinating bird facts - learn about behavior, habitat, and conservation status
- Global birding - explore birds from any region or country worldwide
- Interactive bird identification quiz - test your knowledge and share results
- Viewing history - track every bird you've discovered
- Productivity tools built in - search box, custom shortcuts, clock, and focus timer
- Works offline - enjoy your birds even without internet
- 100% privacy-focused - no personal data collected, no ads, no tracking

POWERED BY EBIRD

All species data comes from eBird (Cornell Lab of Ornithology), the world's largest citizen science biodiversity project, ensuring every photo, call, and fact is accurate and up to date. There are thousands of bird species - from tropical parrots and hummingbirds to majestic eagles and owls.
